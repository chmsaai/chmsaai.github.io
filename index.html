課程簡介

Program Overview

香港珠海學院應用人工智能碩士專業致力於培養具有創新能力和實踐經驗的人工智能專業人才

The Master of Applied Artificial Intelligence program at Hong Kong Chu Hai College is dedicated to cultivating AI professionals with innovative capabilities and practical experience.

本課程結合理論與實踐，涵蓋機器學習、深度學習、自然語言處理、計算機視覺等前沿領域。學生將通過實際項目和案例研究，獲得解決現實世界問題的能力。

課程由資深學者和業界專家授課，提供最先進的實驗設備和豐富的學習資源，為學生在人工智能領域的職業發展奠定堅實基礎。

This program combines theory and practice, covering cutting-edge fields such as machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Students will gain the ability to solve real-world problems through practical projects and case studies.

Taught by experienced scholars and industry experts, the course provides state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and abundant learning resources, laying a solid foundation for students' career development in the field of artificial intelligence.

入學申請

Admission Requirements

歡迎符合以下條件的申請者報讀我們的應用人工智能碩士課程

Applicants who meet the following conditions are welcome to apply for our Master of Applied AI program

基本要求

  • 認可大學的學士學位，計算機科學、工程、數學或相關領域優先
  • 本科學位平均成績不低於B級或同等水平
  • 精通至少一門編程語言（如Python、Java或C++）
  • 英語水平證明（如適用）

申請流程

提交申請

通過在線申請系統提交申請表和所需材料

入學考試

符合條件的申請者將被邀請參加入學考試

面試

通過入學考試的申請者將參加面試

錄取通知

成功通過面試的申請者將在兩週內收到錄取通知

Basic Requirements

  • A bachelor's degree from a recognized university, preferably in computer science, engineering, mathematics or related fields
  • Undergraduate GPA not lower than B grade or equivalent
  • Proficiency in at least one programming language (e.g. Python, Java or C++)
  • Proof of English proficiency (if applicable)

Application Process

Submit Application

Submit application form and required materials through online application system

Entrance Exam

Eligible applicants will be invited to take an entrance exam

Interview

Applicants who pass the entrance exam will be interviewed

Admission Notice

Successful applicants will receive an admission notice within two weeks
